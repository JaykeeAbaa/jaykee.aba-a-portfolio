Brook and Bean Coffee

Warm mornings, better coffee — your neighborhood cafe with locally roasted beans and cozy atmosphere.

Visit website View details

Project Overview

Brook & Bean opened in 2015 with a simple idea: connect our neighborhood over thoughtfully prepared coffee. We partner with local bakers and ethical farms to bring seasonality and care to every cup. Small-batch roasts, house pastries, and friendly faces in a cozy corner for you to pause.

The website showcases their commitment to community, featuring locally roasted coffee, seasonal pour-overs, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The design emphasizes simplicity, sustainability, and the authentic neighborhood coffee shop experience with fresh beans roasted weekly.

Key Features

  • Menu Highlights: Seasonal lattes, single origin espresso, house pastries, and pour-over coffee
  • Order Ahead: Quick pickup and curbside service for busy mornings
  • Community Focus: Monthly open mic nights and local artist collaborations
  • Location & Hours: Daily 7AM-5PM at 123 Maple Avenue with accessible seating
  • Fresh Roasting: Weekly on-site roasting with roast diary and tasting notes

Design Approach

The design captures the warm, welcoming atmosphere of a neighborhood coffee shop. Using earthy tones and clean typography, the website reflects the artisanal quality and community-focused values of Brook & Bean.

The layout emphasizes simplicity and accessibility, making it easy for customers to find menu items, hours, and location information. The responsive design ensures a smooth experience across all devices, from quick mobile orders to desktop browsing.
