Project Overview

Iron Forge is a specialized fitness platform designed for serious athletes and bodybuilders who demand more from their training. This project aimed to create a powerful, professional website that showcases expert-designed programs, advanced tracking tools, and elite coaching staff to help users forge their ultimate physique.

The design emphasizes strength, performance, and scientific precision through bold layouts, powerful imagery, and intuitive navigation. The platform features specialized training programs, progress tracking, form analysis, and expert coaching to deliver results for dedicated athletes pursuing peak performance.

Key Features

  • Specialized Training Programs: Expert-designed workout routines including Power Hypertrophy, Compound Mastery, Shred & Define, and Competition Prep programs
  • Advanced Progress Tracking: Track every rep, set, and weight with detailed analytics to visualize progress over time
  • Form Analysis: Upload videos of lifts and receive expert feedback on technique and form from certified trainers
  • Elite Coaching Staff: World-class athletes and certified specialists including IFBB Pro bodybuilders and IPF World Champions
  • Recovery & Nutrition Integration: Monitor recovery metrics and connect nutrition data to optimize training and meal timing

Design Approach

The visual design emphasizes strength, power, and scientific precision through a bold dark color palette with striking accents that evoke feelings of intensity and performance. The layout prioritizes functionality and data visualization, with clear navigation and prominent call-to-action buttons for program enrollment and training tools.

Typography choices focus on impact and readability, ensuring serious athletes can quickly access critical training information. High-quality imagery showcases elite athletes, proper form demonstrations, and transformation results, building confidence in the platform's expertise and proven methodology.
