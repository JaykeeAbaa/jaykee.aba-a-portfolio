Discover Siargao Island

A travel website showcasing the beauty and attractions of Siargao Island, Philippines

Visit website View details

Project Overview

Siargao Island is a Philippine paradise famed for surfing, clear waters, and lush scenery. This project aimed to create a visually stunning travel website that captures the essence of this tropical destination while providing visitors with essential information about attractions, accommodations, and activities.

The design focuses on immersive imagery, intuitive navigation, and mobile-first responsive design to ensure travelers can easily access information whether they're planning their trip from home or already exploring the island.

Key Features

  • Interactive Island Map: Custom-designed map highlighting key attractions, beaches, and points of interest
  • Accommodation Booking: Integrated booking system for hotels, resorts, and local homestays
  • Activity Planner: Curated list of activities with booking capabilities for surfing lessons, island hopping, and tours
  • Weather Integration: Real-time weather updates and surf conditions
  • Local Guide Section: Insider tips from locals and travel recommendations

Design Approach

The visual design draws inspiration from Siargao's natural beauty - turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and lush tropical vegetation. The color palette features ocean blues and sandy beiges, creating an immersive experience that transports users to the island before they even arrive.

Typography choices emphasize readability while maintaining a relaxed, vacation-inspired feel. Large, high-quality imagery takes center stage, with careful attention to loading optimization for users with varying internet speeds.
