Project Overview

Brook & Bean is a neighborhood coffee shop website that embodies warmth and community spirit. The design creates a welcoming digital experience that reflects the cozy, artisanal atmosphere of a local cafe committed to quality coffee and local partnerships.

The website showcases their dedication to small-batch roasts, house pastries, and friendly service while emphasizing their mission to connect the community over thoughtfully prepared coffee since 2015.

Key Features

  • Menu Integration: Seasonal offerings with detailed pricing and descriptions for espresso, lattes, pastries, and pour-overs
  • Location Services: Complete visit information including hours, accessibility features, and parking details
  • Community Focus: Highlighting local partnerships, open mic nights, and support for neighborhood artists
  • Order Functionality: Order ahead and curbside pickup options for customer convenience
  • Brand Storytelling: Comprehensive about section covering their roasting process, team, and sustainability mission

Design Approach

The visual design emphasizes warmth and approachability through earthy color palettes and clean typography that evokes the comfort of a neighborhood coffee shop. The layout prioritizes essential customer information while maintaining an inviting, community-focused aesthetic.

Typography choices focus on readability and warmth, ensuring customers can quickly access critical information like hours, menu items, and contact details. The responsive design ensures accessibility across all devices, with special attention to mobile users seeking quick information about visiting the cafe.
